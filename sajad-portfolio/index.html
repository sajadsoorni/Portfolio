Projects Showcase

Hometown Page – Kurdistan Tourism

The "Hometown Page" website is a detailed and engaging guide to Sanandaj, a key city in Kurdistan, Iran. The site highlights the city’s rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and the traditional lifestyle of its Kurdish inhabitants. It provides visitors with a glimpse into the city's history, culture, music, and dance through informative sections and vivid imagery. Additionally, the site features an interactive draw system, allowing users to participate in a fun game that offers a chance to win a 60% discount on a Sanandaj tour in March 2025.

Key Features:

  • City Overview: Provides visitors with key information about Sanandaj, including its history, culture, and significance in the Kurdish region.
  • Interactive Draw System: Engages users with a draw game that offers a chance to win discounts on a future tour.
  • Visual Storytelling: Rich images and well-crafted descriptions enhance the user experience, making the site both informative and visually appealing.
  • Tourism Focus: Highlights local attractions and promotes tourism with links to resources like Wikipedia and Tripadvisor for more in-depth exploration.
  • Responsive Design: The site is built with a responsive layout, ensuring compatibility across various devices and screen sizes.

Future Improvements:

  • Multilingual Support: Implement additional languages (e.g., Kurdish and Persian) to cater to a wider audience.
  • Enhanced Booking System: Introduce an integrated booking system for tours directly through the website.
  • User Reviews: Allow visitors to leave feedback and share their experiences after visiting Sanandaj.
  • SEO Optimization: Improve search engine visibility to attract more international tourists.
